Fantasy League Trivia

  • An 8th seed has won a championship 3 times. The winners are Ryan (2012 NBA, 2014 NFL) and JimD (2021 NBA).
  • A 1st seed has won the championship 6 times. The winners are:
    • Watts (2004 NFL)
    • Dell (2008 NFL)
    • Busters (2010 NFL, 2018 NFL)
    • Ryan (2013 NBA, 2023 NFL)
    • Anand (2019 NBA)
    • JimD (2023 NBA)
  • A 1st seed has lost in the championship finals 7 times. The managers who lost are:
    • Thought Criminals (2003 MLB)
    • Dell (2008 NBA)
    • Ryan (2009 MLB)
    • Busters (2009 NFL)
    • Kelvin (2015 NBA)
    • Busters (2016 NBA)
    • TeamQ (2021 NBA)
  • Maureen is the only manager to have won all three leagues in a single year (2005).
  • The longest winning streak is held by JimD, who won the NBA league three years in a row (2021, 2022, and 2023).
